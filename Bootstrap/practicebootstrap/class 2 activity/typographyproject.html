Cricket

is a game between two teams of 11 players on a field at the centre of which is a 
22 yard pitch
with a wicket (a set of three wooden stumps) at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps. The batting side scores runs by striking the ball bowled at the wicket with the bat, while thebowling and fielding side tries to prevent this and
dismiss
each player (so they are "out").
